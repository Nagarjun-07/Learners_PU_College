## Page Sections Breakdown

### Index.html (Home)
- Navigation bar with school logo
- Hero section with inspiring education imagery
- Course Streams (NEET/IIT-JEE/CET/SAT) with CTAs
- Teaching Methodology section with unique approach
- Mentor Exposure program
- Notable Achievers showcase
- Events carousel
- School Clubs grid
- Affiliates section (AOL, ToastMasters, NH, Bagaria Foundation, Educait, L&T)
- Footer with contact info

### About.html
- School overview and mission
- Principal's message
- Faculty profiles and qualifications
- Mandatory disclosures
- Blog section with educational articles

### Courses.html
- Math Foundation Course details
- Complete course catalog with book lists
- Admission criteria and procedures
- Rules and regulations
- Scholarship programs and application process

### Life-at-lgs.html
- Wall of Fame with student achievements
- Campus facilities tour (Labs, Classrooms, Grounds, Canteen, Library, Hostel)
- Events and activities calendar
- Educational tours and excursions
- Photo gallery and media coverage

### Connect.html
- Inquiry form for prospective students
- Career opportunities (teaching positions)
- Newsletter subscription
- Contact information and location

## Interactive Components
1. Course Stream Selector - Interactive cards with hover effects
2. Faculty Profile Cards - Expandable details on click
3. Event Calendar - Monthly view with event details
4. Campus Virtual Tour - Interactive facility explorer
5. Achievement Gallery - Filterable student success stories
6. Contact Forms - Real-time validation and submission

## Design Elements
- Modern educational aesthetic with professional colors
- Easy navigation for parents and students
- Mobile-responsive design
- Smooth animations and transitions
- Accessibility-focused layout
